REVIEW

Kyara McNish

April 14, 2026

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

The Teen Wolf Movie delivers an exciting return to Beacon Hills, offering emotional moments and a sense of closure that many fans had been waiting for. The story feels familiar in a comforting way, bringing back the tone and energy of the original series.

However, the absence of several original characters leaves noticeable gaps in the dynamic that longtime viewers loved. Their missing presence makes parts of the movie feel incomplete, especially during key emotional scenes.

The most surprising choice is the death of Derek Hale, a major character whose loss didn’t feel necessary to the plot. Despite this, the movie remains enjoyable and heartfelt overall, making it a strong and satisfying watch for fans.
