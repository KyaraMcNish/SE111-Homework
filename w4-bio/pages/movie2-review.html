Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Let It Shine is an uplifting movie that blends music, emotion, and storytelling in a way that sits with me til this day.The characters in this movie were only a few years older than me so, it felt The characters feel genuine and relatable, making the story easy to connect with from beginning to end.

As a child, this movie was especially meaningful because I could see myself in the characters—their struggles, their dreams, and their desire to be understood. That personal connection made the story even more powerful and memorable.

The music is one of the film's strongest elements, with songs that are fun, inspiring, and full of energy. Combined with its heartfelt message, Let It Shine remains a fantastic watch and easily earns a full five stars.
