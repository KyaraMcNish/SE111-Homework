REVIEW

Kyara McNish

April 14, 2026

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4/5)

The Teen Wolf Movie is an excting movie that brings it's original series back to the town of Beacon Hills. It provided closure for the fans who believed they never truly got the ending they deserved. The tone and setting of the movie are eerily familar and somewaht comforting.

The absence of several of the orignial characters, however, does leave a noticeable gap in the character dynaimc we know from the orignal series. Due to this many of the scences felt incomplete in an emotional aspect.

The most surprising choice is the death of Derek Hale, a major character whose loss didn’t feel necessary to the plot. Despite this, the movie remains enjoyable and heartfelt overall, making it a strong and satisfying watch for fans.
